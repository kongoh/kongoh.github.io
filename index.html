Planet Rock 2: Electric Boogaloo is a journey through sound and space. Travel through a surreal galaxy, colliding with interstellar sounds to create beautiful music. Wake up and return to a dark and chaotic reality and witness the transformation.

The game is developed by KongOh Games (Kenny Kong and Peter Oh). The game's soundtrack features the freshest galactic funk, soul, and hip-hop from acclaimed musical artists Zion I and DJ Fleg.
